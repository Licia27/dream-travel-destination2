About Zanzibar

Zanzibar is a tropical paradise off the coast of Tanzania, known for its pristine beaches, rich history, and vibrant culture.

Activities in Zanzibar

  • Relax on beautiful white sand beaches
  • Explore the historic Stone Town
  • Go snorkeling or scuba diving in crystal-clear waters
  • Take a spice tour and learn about local agriculture
  • Sail on a traditional dhow boat at sunset
  • Visit the Jozani Forest to see rare red colobus monkeys
